## Supervisor And Research Group

**Supervisor:** Prof. Giorgos Georgiou
**Role:** Group Leader, Ultrafast Quantum Terahertz Nanoelectronics lab
**Email:** giorgos.georgiou@glasgow.ac.uk
**Institution:** University of Glasgow, Electronic and Nanoscale Engineering

Prof. Georgiou is an Associate Professor in Electronic and Nanoscale Engineering at the University of Glasgow. His research expertise includes semiconductor quantum technologies, nanoelectronics, high-frequency electronics and optoelectronics, terahertz technology, photonics, and ultrafast optics.

He leads the Ultrafast Quantum Terahertz Nanoelectronics, or ultraQUTE, lab. The group's research focuses on semiconductor quantum technologies, including semiconductor qubits, operating at very high frequencies and higher cryogenic temperatures around 4 K. The research also explores the integration and scalability of quantum systems with high-frequency terahertz electronics and optoelectronics through smart on-chip photonic packaging.

This MSc project sits within that broader research context by connecting resonator-based qubit readout, high-frequency device behaviour, semiconductor trap dynamics, and simulation-led analysis using Ansys Lumerical.
